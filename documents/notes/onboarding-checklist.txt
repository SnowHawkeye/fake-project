New Engineer Onboarding Checklist
Team: Platform Engineering

--- Day 1 ---
[ ] Laptop setup and access credentials from IT
[ ] Slack workspace invite and channel recommendations (#eng-general, #deployments, #alerts, #random)

[ ] 1:1 with manager to review role and 30/60/90 day goals
