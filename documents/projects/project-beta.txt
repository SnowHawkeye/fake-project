Project Beta - Data Analytics Platform

Status: Planning
Lead: James Okafor
Team: Priya, Leo, Dana

Overview:
Project Beta introduces an internal analytics dashboard to provide real-time insights
into sales, customer behavior, and operational metrics.

Goals:
- Centralize data from 5+ disparate sources
- Enable self-serve reporting for non-technical stakeholders
- Reduce time-to-insight from days to minutes

Dependencies:
- Data warehouse migration (Project Gamma, ETA Q2)
- SSO integration with existing identity provider

Risks:
- Data quality issues in legacy CRM export
- Limited availability of data engineering resources in Q1
